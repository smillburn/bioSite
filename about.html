Ashley Millburn, The wife of Skyler Millburn. She has 2 cats named Neptune and Tigress. She is the second eldest of four siblings. Ashley grew up around games. Both board games and video games. Her father got her started into Dungeons and Dragons where she prefers to play a cleric or paladin. She enjoys playing video games like Borderlands 2, Valheim, and Garry’s Mod with her friends and family. She got into both music and art at a young age. During middle school she played the trumpet in the school band. When she started high school, she changed things up and joined the choir as a soprano. Her senior year of high school she joined the honors choir and competed in some local competitions. She has always enjoyed art. From the classes she took to creating doodles in her free time. In her junior year of high school, she was recognized as part of the school’s district art fair. Ashley spent most of her life growing up in Illinois before moving to Nebraska at the start of her senior year. After high school she spent some time in Utah with her grandma working to save up for college. After moving back home she started college to work on a digital arts degree at metropolitan community college. While going to college she was invited to join Phi Theta Kappa Honor Society. A few months later after moving back to Nebraska she met her husband. The following summer they were married. Ashley looks forward to a future filled with concerts, art exhibits, and games with her friends and family.
